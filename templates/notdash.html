HPV Tweet Analysis

Team Index 12

(HPV) vaccine was first introduced to reduce the infection of HPV and the majority of cervical cancers. there is evidence to suggest that negative information about HPV vaccines from celebrities, health professionals, and news media can increase the HPV vaccine hesitancy and refusal. But the HPV vaccine is still a recent addition to the public health and it is important to perform surveillance on social media to understand various opinions about vaccination.

As one of the serious public health issues, vaccination refusal has been attracting more and more attention, especially for newly approved human papillomavirus (HPV) vaccines. Understanding public opinion towards HPV vaccines is especially a big concern on social media to make strategies for people to take vaccines.
